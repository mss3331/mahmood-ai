Who I am?

I am Mahmood Salah Haithami, a PhD graduate from University of Nottingham, Malaysia Campus. My first exposure to coding was in 2008, when I developed a simple app using JAVA. I liked the concept of bieng able to instruct a machine to do what I wanted! I finished my Bachelor degree in Computer Science in Jordan University of Science and Technology, Jordan and followed by a Master degree from University of Malaya, Malaysia.

My master dissertation was on Digital Image processing, meanwhile, my PhD thesis was on the application of Deep Learning (i.e., Artificial Intellegince) on Colonoscopy. During the period of my academic period, I developed various applications using different languages such as JAVA, C++, Python, MATLAB, JavaScript. The purpose of this website is to demonistrate some of my recent work. Enjoy :)
